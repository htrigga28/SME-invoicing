# Homepage surface brief

- Scope and mode: `/` marketing homepage, Persuade. Shared visual rules also cover the marketing header, footer, privacy, and terms routes.
- Audience and job: Nigerian SME owners and operators deciding whether Lumina can remove manual invoice-payment reconciliation.
- Primary action: join the early-access waitlist. Secondary action: sign in for existing access.
- Proof: accurate interactive product demonstrations and payment-architecture boundaries only; no logos, testimonials, metrics, pricing, or certifications.
- Direction: “The Payment Trail.” A centered owner-facing promise opens into a connected invoice-to-receipt trail, an interactive outcome explorer, a molded operations field, and one trust architecture.
- Memorable moment: a one-time trail draw connects an invoice, Paystack reference, verified match, payout route, and receipt; changing outcome states blurs and shifts the same fixed product shell.
- Constraints: dark/lime Lumina identity, Hanken Grotesk, JetBrains Mono for data only, seven homepage sections, static rendering by default, reduced motion, unchanged waitlist/API contract, and exact preservation of draft legal copy.
